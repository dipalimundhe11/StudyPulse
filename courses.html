Rules & Guidelines

Study Rules

Follow these guidelines for effective learning

Regular Study Schedule

Maintain a consistent study routine. Study at the same time every day to build good habits and discipline.

Daily Revision

Review what you learn each day. Regular revision helps retain information better and strengthens understanding.

Practice Regularly

Solve problems and complete exercises daily. Practice is key to mastering any subject.

Ask Questions

Don't hesitate to ask doubts. Clear your doubts immediately to avoid confusion later.

Use Online Resources

Watch video lessons and download study materials. Make the most of available digital resources.

Stay Healthy

Get enough sleep, eat healthy food, and take regular breaks. A healthy body supports a healthy mind.
